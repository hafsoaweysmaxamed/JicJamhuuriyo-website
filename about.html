ABOUT US

Who are We ?

We act as a focal point for launching and carrying out activities pertaining to professionalism, innovation, andentrepreneurship. We are an ecosystem that exists to support students and young entrepreneurs in their goal to explore creativity and entrepreneurship through stimulating and collaborative environment with multiple successstrategies. We are dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship through dynamic and results-oriented activities.

ABOUT US

WHAT WE DO

We shape the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs. We offer experience and practical learning to youth who quest to start their own enterprises or professions. We provide all the intellectual and material tools innovators and professionals need to grow and prosper, including one-on-one advising, workshops, mentorships, incubator programs, legal guidance, co-working space, pitch competitions, and access to relevant networks. We teach learners how to spin-off an idea and develop new products or modify an existing setup, and we provide them the necessary support to reach the market.

At JIC, we deliver career-enhancing courses and entrepreneurship programs that represent a solid investment in each entrepreneur’s goals and career success. The main task of this center is to build innovator’s skills in the area of entrepreneurship and link them with the community resources that fuel entrepreneurship and business enterprise ownership. Whether you have a passion or curiosity, want to operate a business, or spread your wings with innovative ideas, the center is your starting point to succeed.

Developing learning experiences to enable students and other innovators to be innovative leaders and know how to transform the knowledge, theories, and technologies studied in their disciplines at university for the betterment of society and the economy

Advancing innovation research and practice applications as well as implementing adaptive solutions within the university

ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Jamhuriya Incubation Center (JIC) was founded by Jamhuriya University of Science and Technology (JUST) in 2022. It is an ecosystem that brings together young people from diverse backgrounds to interact, share knowledge experiences, network, and experiment with new ideas, leading to produce valuable products that have a sustainable impact on the whole society.

The objective is to nurture innovation and entrepreneurship culture in the university by training youngsters and other, entrepreneurs to be unique and creative individuals who can think. and evaluate information in new ways, produce new products and services, and come up with creative solutions to social and economic issues.
