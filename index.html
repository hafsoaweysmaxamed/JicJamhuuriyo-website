ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Jamhuriya Incubation Center (JIC) was founded by Jamhuriya University of Science and Technology (JUST) in 2022. It is an ecosystem that brings together young people from diverse backgrounds to interact, share knowledge experiences, network, and experiment with new ideas, leading to produce valuable products that have a sustainable impact on the whole society.

The objective is to nurture innovation and entrepreneurship culture in the university by training youngsters and other, entrepreneurs to be unique and creative individuals who can think. and evaluate information in new ways, produce new products and services, and come up with creative solutions to social and economic issues.

SERVICES

What We Provide

Conductive Physical Environment

The center provides physical facilities such as workspace, electricity, high speed internet access, tools, market research facilities, and conference halls.

Conferences

The center hosts conferences that are intended to enable students and others to participate in key debates and discussions in order to gain a comprehensive understanding of global events.

Consulting Services

Our outstanding staff advisers are ready to support and assist you at any time during the incubation process. Whether you are looking to discuss your plan or venture, learn tools and tactics to speed up your enterprise, practice your pitch, or do anything else, they are here to help.

Teambuilding

We believe working as a team can help youth develop skills that are specific to collaborative efforts, allowing them to tackle more complex problems than they could handle alone.

Workshops

The center offers workshops designed to encourage students to pursue their professional and entrepreneurial aspirations.

Networking

Building networks accelerates the start-ups’ growth and learning process. So, the center provides these networking facilities to its members as it encompasses a set of relationships with various agents and organizations.
